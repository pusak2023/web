About PUSAK

PUSAK is a non-political voluntary organization formed by students of different public universities who are from Kamalganj Upazila. Our mission is to empower youth, support education, and contribute to community development through service, leadership, and collaboration.

Our Activities

Flood Relief

Organizing emergency support and humanitarian activities during natural disasters.

Hosting educational and career-focused sessions for students.
